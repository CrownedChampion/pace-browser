Why Pace

Everything you want, nothing you don't

A modern browser built from the ground up for speed and focus.

⚡

Fast Mode

Pages pre-load as you type in the address bar, so they're ready the instant you hit Enter.

🧩

Chrome extensions

Install your favorite extensions straight from the Chrome Web Store, and pin two to the toolbar.

🛡️

Built-in ad blocking

Ads and cross-site trackers are blocked by default — pages load lighter and cleaner.

🔒

Private by design

Your history, bookmarks, and settings stay on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no selling data.

📱

Sidebar apps

Keep music and chat apps docked in a sidebar — audio keeps playing while you browse.

🔖

Import in seconds

Bring your bookmarks over from Chrome, Edge, Brave, or an HTML export with one click.
