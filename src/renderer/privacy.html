Fast Mode

When Fast Mode is enabled, Pace makes background network requests to websites as you type. These requests originate from your IP and are visible to destination servers. We do not log them. Disable in Settings.

Ad Blocking

Pace's built-in ad blocker filters requests against ad and tracker domain lists stored locally. No browsing data is sent to any filtering service.

Third-Party Extensions & Sidebar Apps

Extensions and sidebar apps operate under their own privacy policies. Pace does not intercept their data. Sidebar apps run in isolated, sandboxed views.

Your Rights

  • View your history at pace://history
  • Clear all data from Settings → Data → Clear Browsing Data
  • Uninstall Pace to remove all local data
