How installing works. Pace now uses a real Chrome Web Store integration: open the store, find an extension, and click Add to Chrome just like in any other browser — or paste a store link / ID above and hit Install. Extensions download, verify, and load automatically, and persist across restarts. Most content and ad-blocking extensions work; some that depend on Chrome-only APIs may not load, and extension toolbar buttons/popups have limited support in the engine. You can also Load Unpacked a folder with a manifest.json.
